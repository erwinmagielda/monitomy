Monitomy Demo Surface

Traffic Signal Monitor

A sample monitored landing page used to generate visits, click activity, bot indicators, and engagement events for the Monitomy PHP/MySQL dashboard.

Event Inputs

Tracked Actions

These controls simulate common landing-page events and submit them to the local logger endpoint.

View Store Records a shop click Subscribe Records a CTA click Play Sample Records an audio click Open Archive Records an archive click Watch Preview Records a video click Contact Records a contact click

Engagement Inputs

Signal Selection

Selecting a signal records a named engagement event using the same album-style format supported by the dashboard.

Output

Private Monitor

The events generated from this demo surface are stored by the PHP logger and reviewed through the Monitomy dashboard.
